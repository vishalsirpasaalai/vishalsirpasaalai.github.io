About Vishal Sirpa Saalai

Vishal SirpaSaalai creates and constructs several Kovil-related products, including Gold Plated Sirpam, Silver Plated Sirpam, Kovil Kalasam, Kovil Nilavu, Prabalayam, Kodi Maram, Gopura Kalasam, Tiruvatchi, Nilavi padi and Aaipon silai. Our extensive experience in this subject stems from years of skilled labor and craftsmanship. In addition to building, we also deliver to many locations. Regarding Kovil Sirpam, please get in touch with Vishal SirpaSaalai.

MISSION STATEMENT

At Vishal SirpaSaalai, our mission is to preserve and promote the rich tradition of temple craftsmanship by creating exquisite Kovil-related products, including Gold Plated Sirpam, Silver Plated Sirpam, Kovil Kalasam, Kovil Nilavu, Prabalayam, Kodi Maram, and Aaipon Silai. Through our dedication to skilled artistry, precision, and devotion, we strive to enhance the spiritual and aesthetic essence of temples while ensuring the highest quality and authenticity in every creation.

Goals

  • 1. Excellence in Craftsmanship: Uphold the highest standards of artistry and precision in creating Kovil Sirpam and temple-related artifacts.

  • 2. Cultural Preservation: Preserve and promote traditional temple craftsmanship for future generations.

  • 3. Widespread Reach: Expand our services to temples across various locations to make our handcrafted products more accessible.

  • 4. Quality and Authenticity: Maintain superior quality and authenticity in all our creations using the finest materials.

  • 5. Customer Satisfaction: Build lasting relationships with temple authorities and devotees by offering exceptional products and services.
